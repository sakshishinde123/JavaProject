PRINCIPAL’S MESSAGE

Dear Students,

Welcome to Samarth College of Pharmacy (SCOP). I heartily congratulate you for opting Pharmacy as course to shape up your professional career and personal lives.


Samarth College of Pharmacy provides two years Diploma in Pharmacy (D. Pharm.) Course and four years of Bachelor in Pharmacy degree (B. Pharmacy) course. Pharmacy as a profession comprises as vital part of health care system in India and across the world.


The institute has a state of the art infrastructure, well equipped ultra-modern and spacious laboratories, highly qualified experienced and dedicated faculty, well stocked library, hostels, canteen and transportation facility. It also has a placement cell which plays a key role towards appropriate and timely placement of eligible students as per student’s caliber in various Governments, semi government, Private and Pharmacy/non Pharmacy sectors.


In addition to this, the college organizes training sessions, guest lectures, seminars, workshops, sports events and cultural activities etc. for the all round development of each and every student. The conduction of soft skill training sessions by the professionals is an attempt to help students become job ready candidates for the competent Pharmacy/ Non Pharmacy sector. The institute also trains the students in Communication Skills through English Language Laboratories. The faculty members are supported for knowledge and skill enhancement and updating via teaching and learning program, seminar, workshop, publication of Research and Review articles etc.


To lead the team of teachers and students, I have a versatile background as I have completed my post graduation in Pharmaceutical Biotechnology and Ph.D. in Pharmaceutical Sciences. I have Postdoctoral research experience at Government National and International laboratories in the field of Defence sector and, credits of more than 20 articles in reputed journals and has serves as a reviewer of repute. Research experience is in the field of in vitro/ in vivo immunology assays, techniques studies with the help of flow cytometry, immune based Herbal Medicinal plants, Gentoxicity studies which shall contribute to the area of advancement.

Thanks for the visit!

Dr.Ghule S.D.
       Principal
