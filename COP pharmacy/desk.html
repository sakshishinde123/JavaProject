PRESIDENT’S DESK

Pharmacy as a profession comprises as integral part of health care system in India & across the world. It gives me a great pleasure to introduce our Institute, Samarth Rural Educational Institute’s “Samarth College of Pharmacy”, Belhe, (Pune) which is established in the year 2019 for Diploma in Pharmacy (D. Pharmacy) and Bachelor in Pharmacy (B. Pharmacy) has been started in the year 2019. The institute has a state of the art infrastructure, well equipped modern and spacious laboratories, highly qualified experienced faculty, well stocked Library, Hostel and Canteen facility. We have a vision to become a leading center of Pharmaceutical Education and Research. Our institute is also in process of forming a collaborative research activities / projects with Pharmacy and non Pharmacy institute. All activities are guided and supported by Honorable Shri. Vallabh Shelke, Executive Director of the institute. I am sure that with support of employee and students we will achieve our goals and program policy.

Hon. Mr. Vivek V. Shelke
Secretory
